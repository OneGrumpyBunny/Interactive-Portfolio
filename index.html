Learn more about me

I'm passionate about three things: quality, elegance and effenciency, which is the essence of every successful website project. It's this passion for elegant design and my talent for web development that equips me with the ability to provide a superior product and service to my employers and their clients.

I work alongside talented designers to produce a full-featured, engaging website experience. As lead developer, I successfully manage the full life-cycle of website projects including, concept, strategy, coding, version control, user testing, and ultimately - deployment.

With the ever changing landscape of web development, I pick up new techniques and apply them to current projects whenever possible. As a lifelong learner, I am self-taught through online courses, self-paced study and old-fashioned elbow grease. Nearly all of my programming knowledge comes from self-paced study.

My career began at an Internet Service Provider in the mid 90's. Since then, I have held various titles including System Administrator, Lead Developer, and UX Designer. My talents are constantly evolving, and my skillset grows with each new technique I learn. Currently, I'm studying web animation.

Outside of professional life, I exercise my creative muscles by crafting, gardening, sewing, painting, photography, you name it. I love DIY and working with my hands. I try to learn a new skill with each new year. This year, it was learning how to sew. I did it, and I loved it.
